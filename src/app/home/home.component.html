Welcome to Create Your Story app!

Did you already had a lack of inspiration? How to start your story? Or do you simply want to try to have some fun? This app is made for you

C.Y.S will stimulate your creativity and imagination. Two rule are available : Alphabetic and Category. By choosing Alphabetic, you will have to chose one or more letter where to pick up the words and same for the Category one or more (such as Aventure, Horror, Romantic,...)

Follow the instructions for each rule. You will obtain a list of word. Then create your story with them.

This app is inspired by the board game : Aye, Dark Overlord!
